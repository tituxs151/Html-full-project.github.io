Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 1 kg
  • Rice - 2 cups
  • Spices (Turmeric, Cumin, Garam Masala)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  2. Cook the rice separately.
  3. Layer chicken and rice in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat for 30 minutes.

Nutrition Info

Calories
500 kcal per serving
Protein
30g

Kacchi Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • Mutton - 1 kg
  • Basmoti Rice - 1/2 kg
  • Oil
  • Tomato Sos
  • Alu Bukhara
  • Medium size potato 8-10
  • Yogurt
  • Salt
  • Soya sos
  • Spices (Turmeric, Cumin, Garam Masala)

Steps

  1. Marinate the Mutton with spices and other ingredients .
  2. Cook the rice separately almost 70%.
  3. Layer Mutton and rice in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat for 40 minutes.

Nutrition Info

Calories
1300-1400 kcal per serving
Protein
40g

Payesh Recipe

Ingredients

  • Polao Rice - 1/2 cup
  • Full cream Milk - 1 Litter
  • Ghee - 3 tablespoon
  • Cardamom - 3/4 gm
  • Alamonds - 10 Sliced

Steps

  1. Rinse rice and soak it in 2+ 1/2 cups of water for 20 minutes and drain.
  2. Take a saucepan and pour milk into it and keep stirring.Turn the gas flame to medium-high and keep stirring. Boil the Milk with cardamoms.Once the milk starts reducing, turn the flame to low and rice and simmer the milk and rice untill it is reduced to almost half.
  3. Add sugar and cook for another couple of minutes.Turn of the flame and garnish with almonds.serve warm or chilled.

Nutrition Info

Calories
420 kcal per serving
Protein
11.2 g
